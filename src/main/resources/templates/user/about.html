WHO WE ARE

We are an online ticketing website designed for events and festivals. We founded our website in 2023. We are based in Yangon, Myanmar, and one of the best ticketing sites running currently in our country.

WHAT WE DO

We provide ticket selling and buying services to our customers. We keep ourselves updated with the current trends of online ticketing services and actively update our website to meet the customer needs.

WHY WE DO

We founded our website with the purpose to let our customers buy or sell tickets fast and easy. We aim to be able to stand as a household name when it comes to online ticketing sites in our country.

OUR STORY

We wanted to build a website that is user-friendly and can contribute to our community and came to a decision to design a ticketing website aimed for events and festivals, since there are not many such sites in our country. After working on this website for about a month, our website is now up and running and becoming a widely-used, convenient-to-use online ticking site for our customers.

OUR TEAM

Our team includes five members in total. We are all programming students living in Yangon, Myanmar. We are actively making sure our website is running smoothly and our customers are having best customer experiences using it. We immediately solve the problems that may arise from ticket buyers or sellers while using our services.

WHY SHOULD CHOOSE US
Trustworthy

We are an authentic website and you can trust us with your personal data as we priortise our customers' privacy. We actively check our website to make sure no fraud is going on, on either buyers' or sellers' sides.

Responsive

Simplicity
